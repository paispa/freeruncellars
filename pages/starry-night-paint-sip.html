The Painting Process
How we'll paint
your Starry Night
1
Draw your gold outlines

Using a gold outliner pen, you'll trace the composition onto your glass — the rolling hills, swirling sky, and moon circle. This is what gives the finished piece its beautiful stained-glass look.

2
Fill the night sky

Using navy and cobalt blue acrylic, you'll dab and swirl to fill the sky sections. We'll guide you through the Van Gogh swirling technique — it's easier than it looks.

3
Paint the moon

Yellow center fading to white, with a golden halo. This is the centerpiece of your composition.

✦ Use the tip of your brush handle to dot the stars
4
Dark hills & teal water

Deep green-black for the rolling hills, sweeping teal strokes for the water below. This contrast is what makes the night sky pop.

5
Final touches & take it home

Add any finishing stars, step back, and admire what you made. Your hand-painted glass goes home with you that night.

✦ Bake at 325°F for 40 min at home to make it dishwasher safe
