To our most cherished friends,

We didn't just buy a winery. We bought a home — four acres of Pinot Gris vines, a walnut grove, a pond full of turtles, and a story that was waiting to be continued. We moved in, put down roots, and started making wine that is genuinely, entirely ours.

The Owners Circle is our way of bringing a handful of people along for the ride — not as customers, but as fellow travelers in this journey. You'll know what we know, when we know it. First access to wines straight from our vines. A quiet seat at the table before the doors open to the public.

You pay an annual membership. We give you $150 in Free Run Credits right back — to spend on wine, tastings, events, or merchandise. After that, everything is discounted. And when Free Run performs well, we intend to share a piece of that back with you too.

If you visit us more than a few times a year, this pays for itself. Beyond the savings, you become part of something closer — connected to the vineyard, the wine, and the people behind it.

Trish & Prashanth
