Before you arrive
A few things worth knowing
🐾
Dogs & families welcome
Well-behaved dogs are welcome on the patio and grounds. Kids are welcome too — we have a play structure and plenty of open space to run around in.
🍽
No outside food, please
We offer packaged snacks including crackers, cheese, meat sticks, chips, and kids' snacks. Non-alcoholic beverages are also available. Outside food is not permitted.
🚗
Designated drivers
Non-alcoholic beverages are complimentary for designated drivers. We appreciate you looking out for your group.
♪
Live music every Sunday
Live music runs 3:00–5:00 PM every Sunday, March through October. Half off glass pours from open bottles during the set. Always free admission.
