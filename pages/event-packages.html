Why Free Run

A setting that does
half the work

Some venues make you bring the magic. At Free Run, it's already here — four acres of Pinot Gris vines, a pond that catches the evening light, a stone patio strung with lights, and a team that genuinely wants your event to be unforgettable.

We host just two or three large events each year. That means when we say yes to your event, we mean it — you get our full attention, not a slot in a conveyor belt.

Whether you're planning a wedding, a milestone birthday, a rehearsal dinner, or a corporate gathering, we'll work with you to make it feel exactly right.
