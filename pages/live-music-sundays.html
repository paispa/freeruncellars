What to Expect

Two hours of music,
and nowhere to be

Every Sunday from March through October, we open the patio to local and regional artists who bring something real to the afternoon. No cover, no fuss — just good music in a setting that makes it impossible to leave early.

When the weather turns, we move things inside. Either way, the music plays.

  • 🗓
    Season
    March through end of October — every Sunday without exception.
  • ⏰
    Time
    3:00 – 5:00 PM. Doors open at 2:00 PM — come early for the best seats on the patio.
  • 🎟
    Admission
    Always free — no cover, no catch. Grab a glass if you like, or just grab a seat. Occasional ticketed concerts (handled by the artist) will be noted on the events calendar.
  • 🌧
    Rain or Shine
    Outdoor patio when weather allows. We move inside for rain or cold — music goes on regardless.

Good to Know

Before you arrive

🐾
Dogs Welcome

Well-behaved dogs are always welcome on the grounds and patio. Please clean up after your pets. On cold or rainy days we'll often make exceptions to come inside too.

🧀
Food & Snacks

We carry packaged snacks — crackers, cheese, meat sticks, chips, and kid-friendly options. Outside food is not permitted, as we want to keep the grounds clean for everyone.

👨‍👩‍👧
Family Friendly

Kids are always welcome. We have a play area and open lawns to roam. Sunday afternoons at Free Run are a family affair — yours included.

🎤
Ticketed Concerts

Occasionally we host special ticketed performances. These are managed directly by the artist. Check the events calendar or follow us on Facebook for announcements.
