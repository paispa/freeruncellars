About Prashanth and Trish

They are parents to 3 young children, #3under3, with impressive careers in the tech and healthcare industries, respectively. They love to travel, eat good food, and build Legos during the holidays.

Prashanth, originally from South India, grew up roaming his family’s coffee estates. Trish, an Indiana native, is making her way back to her roots. Moving from Colorado, their little explorers are ready to enjoy all the fruits Southwest Michigan has to offer!
